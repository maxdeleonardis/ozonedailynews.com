# 📜 MANIFESTO

## ObjectWire: The Future of News

---

### Vision Statement

**ObjectWire is building the world's most interactive news reading experience paired with the world's easiest publishing tool.**

We believe that:
- News should be **verified**, not viral
- Reading should be **engaging**, not exhausting
- Publishing should be **effortless**, not engineering
- Sources should be **transparent**, not buried
- Facts should be **first**, not forgotten

---

## I. The Problem We Solve

### For Readers:
The news is broken. Traditional media is dying while misinformation thrives. Readers face:
- **Information overload** without verification
- **Passive reading experiences** that don't engage
- **Hidden sources** that can't be verified
- **Cluttered interfaces** designed for ads, not understanding
- **No way to know** what's fact vs. opinion

### For Writers:
Publishing is unnecessarily hard. Journalists and creators struggle with:
- **Complex CMS systems** that require training
- **No built-in verification tools**
- **Disconnected workflows** between writing and publishing
- **Technical barriers** to multimedia content
- **SEO as an afterthought**, not integrated

---

## II. What We're Building

### 🌐 The Interactive News Portal

**For Readers — A new way to consume news:**

1. **Animated Data Stories**
   - Statistics that count up as you scroll
   - Timelines that unfold before your eyes
   - Comparisons that animate side-by-side
   - Key points that build section by section

2. **Source Transparency**
   - Every claim linked to its origin
   - One-click verification of sources
   - Primary sources, not aggregators
   - Clear distinction: fact vs. analysis

3. **Topic Hubs**
   - Deep dives like our `/iran` coverage hub
   - Curated timelines of developing stories
   - Real-time updates with verification badges
   - Cross-linked related reporting

4. **Progressive Reading**
   - Content reveals as you scroll
   - Key terms highlighted for skimmers
   - Executive summaries for busy readers
   - Deep dives for the curious

5. **Trust Indicators**
   - Verification badges on claims
   - Author expertise visible
   - Editorial process transparent
   - Corrections prominently displayed

### ✏️ The Effortless Publishing Tool

**For Writers — The world's easiest news CMS:**

1. **Block-Based Writing**
   - 13+ content types, one-click add
   - Drag-and-drop reordering
   - No code, no complexity
   - What you see is what readers get

2. **Built-In Verification**
   - Source citation workflow
   - Fact-checking checkboxes
   - Required fields for transparency
   - Pre-publish validation

3. **SEO as Default**
   - Real-time title/description scoring
   - Google News optimization built-in
   - Structured data automatic
   - Schema markup invisible to writers

4. **Multimedia Made Simple**
   - Drag-drop image uploads
   - Video embeds from URL
   - Stat grids that auto-animate
   - Timelines that self-build

5. **Zero Learning Curve**
   - If you can write an email, you can publish
   - No training required
   - No documentation needed
   - Publish in minutes, not hours

## IV. Our Standards

### Editorial Principles

1. **Accuracy Over Speed**
   - We don't chase breaking news
   - We verify before we publish
   - Getting it right > getting it first

2. **Primary Sources Only**
   - We cite origins, not aggregators
   - Every claim must be traceable
   - Readers can verify independently

3. **Transparent Corrections**
   - Errors are corrected publicly
   - Changes are timestamped
   - Original text preserved in notes

4. **Clear Separation**
   - News vs. Opinion labeled
   - Sponsored content disclosed
   - Editorial independence protected

5. **Accountability**
   - Authors named and contactable
   - Editorial process documented
   - Ownership transparent
